PIC – Event Organizer DSC

Soft Skill 2023–2024

Served as **PIC (Person In Charge)** leading a team of 10 activists in organizing and executing 3 major DSC events: • DSC Kind – social visit to an orphanage • DSC WDB – community iftar and movie night • Smash & Dash – DSC badminton tournament

🌟 Impact

  • Coordinated 10 activists to successfully run 3 different events
  • Brought social, cultural, and sports engagement to the DSC community
  • Balanced teamwork, logistics, and creativity under one leadership role

💡 Skills

  • 👥 Leadership
  • 📅 Event Management
  • 🤝 Team Collaboration
  • ⚡ Problem Solving
  • 🔥 Motivation & Coordination
  • 🎯 Strategic Planning

📌 Reflection

Acting as PIC taught me how to manage a diverse team, delegate tasks effectively, and ensure multiple events run smoothly. Each event had unique challenges — from logistics in charity work, managing atmosphere in social gatherings, to organizing sports tournaments. This experience sharpened my leadership, adaptability, and event management skills.
