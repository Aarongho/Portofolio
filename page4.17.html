Education in Indonesia 2022

Data Visualization / R 2025

A data visualization project on Indonesia’s education quality in 2022. Includes teacher and library distribution, participation trends, and education–poverty correlation.

🎯 What I Learned

  • Visualized disparities in teacher and library distribution
  • Analyzed school participation across age groups
  • Showed correlation between education and poverty

🛠 Tech Stack

  • R, Shiny, bslib
  • ggplot2, plotly
  • dplyr, readxl

📌 Evaluation / Next Step

The visualizations clearly show that Indonesia has improved education participation since 2002, but disparities remain in teacher and library distribution. Provinces with fewer resources struggle with educational outcomes, reinforcing poverty cycles. Next steps include adding real-time data sources, more indicators such as dropout rates, and predictive models to support SDG 4 targets for 2030.
